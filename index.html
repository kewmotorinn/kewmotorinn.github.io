DIRECTIONS

From the Bronx – take Whitestone Bridge to Cross Island Pkwy south. Continue on to Grand Central Parkway West. Take exit 17, then stay on service road approx. 7min. Hotel will be on the right.

From west (Manhattan) – take Grand Central East to exit14 (Union Tpke). Motel is right off the exit.

From East (Long Island) – take GC west to exit 17, stay on service road for approx. 7min, motel is on right hand side, big white bldg.

Subway – E or F train to Kew Gardens/Union Tpke stop. Exit station, walk towards Union Tpke (15min.walk) or hail a taxi.

Bus – Q46 to Main street and Union tpke.
